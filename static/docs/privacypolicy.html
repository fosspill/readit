Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Collection and Usage

We adhere to a strict data minimization policy:

  • We only collect information that you provide directly to us, such as:
    • Your chosen nickname
    • Books you are reading
  • We do not automatically log any personal data about your use of our service.
  • Connection logs, which may contain technical information but no personal data, are maintained solely for debugging purposes and are automatically wiped daily.

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your personal data:

  • All data is stored securely on servers located in the European Union
  • All data is accessed only by authorized personnel
  • All sensitive data is hashed and salted before storage
  • We follow privacy-by-design principles in all our operations

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Rights related to automated decision-making and profiling

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. Connection logs are deleted daily.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and if significant changes are made, we will notify you through our service.
